Arcane Creators

Alex Yee & Christian Linke

It's hard to answer how we did it, but at the end of the day, the most important thing was just to tell a good story, and we felt like a good story wouldn't require you to come in with knowledge in advance. We spent a lot of time trying to strike a balance of setting up characters and trying to find ways to keep that setup engaging and suspenseful, even though for a lot of players, they're like, 'I know where they're going to wind up.'' As for why we decided to make it more accessible. I think for a lot of us who have played League for a really long time, a lot of our friends and family have no idea what the game's world is. So, we really wanted players to be able to share the world they've been spending so much time in with people around them, and have this way of having a conversation with people outside of the League fandom.
